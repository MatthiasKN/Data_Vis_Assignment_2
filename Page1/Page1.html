Purpose

Climate change is a contentious issue, an although there is scientific consensus that human activity is a primary cause, it can be difficult to explain and visualize climate change in the context of the earth’s long and complex climate history. The climate has changed in the past without human influence, and there are several other factors which could conceivably drive climate change. This project attempts to provide a visual answer to the question: “What’s really warming the world?”
